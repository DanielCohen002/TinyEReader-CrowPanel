EPUB → TXT converter

Converts an EPUB to the plain-.txt format this reader's firmware expects, with chapter markers inserted at real headings. Runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Note: if you're uploading to a CrowPanel TinyEReader directly, you don't need this page — its own upload screen converts EPUBs automatically. This page is for converting ahead of time, or for the Heltec build, which doesn't have on-device conversion.
